## What Causes Mold?

Mold, a primeval organism thrives in damp, humid conditions. It could be a result of water leaking into your home, or a product of nature’s furry in the form of floods – prevalent in Miami, Florida. This silent predator lurks behind walls, in bathrooms, and anywhere with moisture and poor sunlight. Hence the importance of water damage control and management.
