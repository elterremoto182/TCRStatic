## Cost Factors That Affect Your Bill

### 1. Water Category (Type of Water)

The type of water involved significantly impacts cost:

**Category 1 - Clean Water** (Least expensive)
- Source: Broken supply lines, faucets, appliances with clean water
- Health risk: Low
- Cost impact: Base pricing

**Category 2 - Gray Water** (Moderate cost increase)
- Source: Washing machines, dishwashers, toilet overflow (urine only)
- Health risk: Moderate
- Cost impact: 20-40% higher than Category 1

**Category 3 - Black Water** (Most expensive)
- Source: Sewage, rising flood water, standing water with bacteria
- Health risk: High - requires specialized handling
- Cost impact: 50-100%+ higher than Category 1

### 2. Water Damage Class (Extent of Damage)

**Class 1 - Minor**
- Small area affected
- Minimal absorption
- Example: Toilet overflow in bathroom
- Cost: $1,000 - $3,000

**Class 2 - Significant**
- Large area affected
- Water wicked up walls less than 24"
- Example: Water heater failure
- Cost: $3,000 - $8,000

**Class 3 - Extensive**
- Water from overhead
- Ceilings, walls, insulation, subfloor saturated
- Example: Upstairs pipe burst
- Cost: $8,000 - $18,000

**Class 4 - Specialty**
- Water trapped in materials like hardwood, stone, concrete
- Requires special drying techniques
- Example: Slab leak affecting foundation
- Cost: $15,000 - $35,000+

### 3. Square Footage Affected

General pricing per square foot in Broward County:

| Service | Cost Per Square Foot |
|---------|---------------------|
| Water extraction | $3 - $7 |
| Structural drying | $4 - $10 |
| Dehumidification | $25 - $75/day per unit |
| Carpet removal/disposal | $2 - $4 |
| Drywall removal | $1 - $3 |
| Antimicrobial treatment | $2 - $4 |

### 4. Materials Affected

Different materials require different approaches:

**Carpet and Padding**
- Cleaning (if salvageable): $3-5/sq ft
- Replacement (if necessary): $8-15/sq ft installed

**Hardwood Floors**
- Drying (if caught early): $5-10/sq ft
- Refinishing: $3-8/sq ft
- Replacement: $8-20/sq ft

**Drywall**
- Drying (if minimal damage): $2-4/sq ft
- Removal and replacement: $2-6/sq ft

**Insulation**
- Usually requires replacement if wet
- Cost: $1-3/sq ft

### 5. Time Factor

The longer water sits, the higher the cost:

- **0-24 hours**: Standard restoration costs
- **24-48 hours**: Add 10-20% for beginning mold risk
- **48-72 hours**: Add 20-40% + likely mold remediation
- **72+ hours**: Significantly higher costs, extensive mold treatment

## Insurance and Water Damage Costs

### What's Typically Covered

Most Broward County homeowner's policies cover:
- Sudden, accidental water damage
- [Burst pipe damage](/problems/burst-pipe-water-damage/fort-lauderdale/)
- Appliance failures
- Storm wind-driven rain (through damaged roof)

### What's Typically NOT Covered

- Gradual leaks and seepage
- Maintenance neglect
- Flood damage (requires separate policy)
- Ground water

### Your Out-of-Pocket Costs

Even with insurance:
- **Deductible**: Typically $500-2,500 (or percentage for hurricane)
- **Limits**: Some policies cap water damage coverage
- **Depreciation**: Actual cash value policies pay less than replacement cost

## How to Save on Water Damage Restoration

### Do's

✓ **Act fast** - Every hour saved reduces drying time and prevents mold

✓ **Get multiple estimates** - Compare at least 2-3 companies

✓ **Ask about insurance billing** - Direct billing saves you cash flow issues

✓ **Request itemized estimates** - Know exactly what you're paying for

### Don'ts

✗ **Don't choose solely on price** - Cheapest isn't always best

✗ **Don't skip mold prevention** - Remediation later costs much more

✗ **Don't DIY extensive damage** - Improper drying causes bigger problems
