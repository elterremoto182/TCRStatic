Disasters don't wait for business hours. Our emergency restoration team is available around the clock, every day of the year. Whether you're dealing with a burst pipe at midnight or storm damage on a holiday weekend, we're ready to respond.

Total Care Restoration proudly serves residential and commercial properties throughout South Florida, including Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. Our local team can typically reach your property within 30-60 minutes for emergency situations.
