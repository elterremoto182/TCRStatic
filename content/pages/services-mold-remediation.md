Remediation involves the process of eliminating materials and mold. Mold removal specifically refers to getting rid of mold from surfaces without removing the underlying material. On the other hand, sanitization focuses on treating both the home and the air to ensure cleanliness and hygiene.

The process of mold remediation service involves several steps. First, a thorough inspection of the property is conducted.
